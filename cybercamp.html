    • Cyber Camp 2018

    Where high school students equip themselves with cyber security skills!

    Purpose

    Designed for high school students who want to learn about cyber security. They will get the opportunity to complete a week of training in cybersecurity by highly skilled trainers from City College of San Francisco. Then, students compete against other schools in real-time to secure Windows and Linux systems quickly and accurately.
