    Rules
    1. Don't attack the scoreboard or infrastructure. Just don't.
    2. If you solve a challenge, do not share the flag or give hints to others.
    3. Do not sabotage other teams or hinder their progress.
    4. Please do not spam the problem writers for hints.
    5. Have fun! Try to learn as much as possible!
