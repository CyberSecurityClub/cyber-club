    Purpose

    Designed for high school students who want to learn about cyber security. They will get the opportunity to complete a week of training in cybersecurity by highly skilled trainers from City College of San Francisco.

    Students compete against other schools in real-time to secure Windows and Linux systems quickly and accurately.
